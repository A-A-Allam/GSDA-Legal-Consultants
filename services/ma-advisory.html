Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for an M&A transaction?

M&A transactions typically range from 6-12 months depending on complexity, regulatory requirements, and market conditions. Our streamlined process ensures efficient execution while maintaining thoroughness.

How do you handle cross-border M&A transactions?

We leverage our international presence in Egypt, France, and GCC markets to navigate complex cross-border regulations, tax implications, and cultural considerations effectively.

What makes GSDA different from other M&A advisors?

Our unique combination of legal expertise, industry knowledge, and strategic business acumen positions us as true partners in your M&A journey, not just service providers.

Do you work with private equity firms?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with private equity firms on both buy-side and sell-side transactions, including portfolio company acquisitions and exits.

What is your approach to deal valuation?

We employ multiple valuation methodologies including DCF analysis, comparable company analysis, and precedent transactions to ensure comprehensive and accurate valuations.

How do you ensure confidentiality during the process?

All communications are protected by attorney-client privilege, and we implement strict confidentiality protocols including NDAs and secure data handling procedures.
